The Effective Learning Environments Observation Tool (eleot)

Experience the power of a learner-centric observation tool that measures and quantifies active student engagement by taking a look at classroom expectations, feedback and learning support.

The eleot focuses on seven key learning environments that promote effective learning and student success:

  • equitable learning
  • high expectations
  • supportive learning
  • active learning
  • progress monitoring and feedback
  • well-managed learning
  • digital learning
  • Download Whitepaper
  • FAQ

eleot empowers you to:

  • Evaluate classroom environments by focusing on students
  • Reveal strengths and weaknesses using measurable data
  • Identify trends by comparing observations across subjects, grade levels and other filters
  • Ensure quality and reliability in an intuitive and easy-to-use tool
  • Implement a powerful tool for professional development, peer learning and ongoing improvement

Features

A school’s connectivity is never a barrier.

A school’s connectivity is never a barrier. Conduct observations online or offline and upload later.

Record key learnings from your observations.

Capture important details by taking notes as you go. Observations and notes are seamlessly organized.

Become a certified observer.

Establish reliability and ensure the validity of your observation data by passing the eleot certification online exam. Asynchronous online training is provided with every license.
